2010: san diego

ashley is a designer. she was starting at a new company where she heard about this amazing engineer who she thought she could nerd out with...little did she know how cute he was.

ross is an engineer. he had been working at a tech company in san diego for a little over a year when he noticed a cute new girl joined and was determined to get her on a date.

ross asked ashley to the movies, but since she didn't want to date coworkers, she told him she had to go grocery shopping. however ross's persistence won and a short while later they started dating.

2011: berkeley

after a year of dating, ross and ashley decided to further their tech careers in the bay area. they traded the beach for a cottage in the woods of north berkeley.

2012: san francisco

after a year and a half in berkeley, they finally found an affordable loft in potrero hill and moved to san francisco.

2013: abroad

after taking many trips to new states and up-and-down the california coast, ross and ash finally took their first trip overseas to france and ireland. ash got to see what ross's life was like when he studied abroad.

2014: engagement

ross and ash walk to work together and have a morning date at least once a week. one morning they were up early, so he suggested they have a long breakfast and coffee date at their favorite restaurant. instead of their usual route, ross asked ashley to walk by the water...

ross said he had to tie his shoe and when ashley looked down, he was on one knee, ring in hand and asked her to marry him. they sat on a bench soaking it all in and now they have their bench by the water.
