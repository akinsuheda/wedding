2010: san diego

ashley is a designer. she was starting at a new company where she heard about this amazing engineer who she thought she could nerd out with...little did she know how cute he was.

ross is an engineer. he had been working at a tech company in san diego for a little over a year when he noticed a cute new girl joined and was determined to get her on a date.

ashley wasn't keen on dating anyone at work. ross asked her to the movies and she told him she had to go grocery shopping. a short while later she couldn't resist and they started dating.

2011: berkeley

after a year of dating, ross and ashley decided to pursue Silicon Valley and the tech scene there. they traded the beach in for a cottage in the woods of north berkeley.

2012: san francisco

they moved to san francisco after a year and a half in berkeley to try living in the city they love. they moved to a loft in potrero hill and became city people.

2013: abroad

taking many trips to new states and up-and-down the california coast, ross and ash finally took thier first trip over seas to france and ireland. ash got to see what ross' life was like when he studied abroad.

2014: color

ross and ash walk to work together and have a morning date at least once a week. one morning they were up early, so ross suggested they have a long breakfast and coffee date at our favorite places. ross asked ashley to walk by the water and she thought man, this is such a great morning - she wished every day could start like this. little did she know that it was going to get even better...

ross said he had to tie his shoe and when ashley looked at him to see why he was down there so long, he was on one knee, ring in hand and asked to if she'd marry him. they sat on a bench for awhile soaking it all in and now they have their special bench that they walk by every day.
